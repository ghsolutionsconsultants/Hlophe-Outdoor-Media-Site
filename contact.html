Common Questions

Our current network is focused on Gauteng's township and urban markets, with concentration in Soweto and surrounding areas. We are actively expanding our site network across the broader Gauteng province. Contact us to discuss coverage in specific areas.

Typically 5–10 business days from artwork sign-off for static billboards and spaza branding. Digital OOH campaigns can go live within 48–72 hours once artwork is approved. Timelines for wall murals vary based on scale and complexity.

We'll provide site-specific artwork specifications after you book. Generally, we require high-resolution print-ready files (PDF or TIFF, minimum 300 DPI at output size) for static formats. Digital screens accept standard video formats (MP4) and static images (JPG/PNG) at screen resolution.

Our standard minimum for static billboards and spaza branding is one month. Digital OOH campaigns can be booked for shorter durations. Wall murals are typically booked for a minimum of three months given the production investment involved. We're flexible — contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

While we don't offer in-house design as a standard service, we can connect you with trusted creative partners who specialise in outdoor advertising artwork — particularly designers experienced in township market aesthetics and high-impact OOH creative.

We invoice on a monthly basis with payment terms of 30 days from invoice date for approved clients. A deposit may be required for new clients or production-intensive formats such as wall murals. We accept EFT. Contact us for a detailed rate card and terms.
