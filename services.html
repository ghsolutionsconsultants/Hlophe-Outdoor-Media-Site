Static Billboard
Format 01

Static Billboards

Our large-format static billboards are positioned at the highest-traffic nodes in Gauteng's township corridors — delivering continuous, unmissable brand visibility 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Each site is carefully selected based on pedestrian footfall, vehicle traffic counts, sightline distances, and proximity to commercial anchors such as shopping centres, taxi ranks, and community hubs.

Prime Locations
Sites near Jabulani Mall, Bara Taxi Rank, Protea Glen, Meadowlands and key commuter routes throughout Soweto and greater Gauteng.
24/7 Exposure
Illuminated panels ensure your brand remains visible morning, afternoon, and night — capturing commuters across all shifts.
Proven Reach
Trusted by brands including Colgate, Old Mutual, Cell C, Hyundai, and ABSA to deliver consistent township market visibility.
Community Investment
Many sites are co-located at schools and places of worship — your rental investment supports community institutions.

Spaza Shop Branding
Format 02

Spaza Shop Branding

South Africa has over 150,000 spaza shops serving as daily touchpoints for township communities. Hlophe Outdoor Media transforms these trusted local spaces into powerful, branded environments that drive recognition and affinity.

Spaza branding delivers something no billboard can — the endorsement of a trusted community institution. When consumers see your brand on their local spaza, it becomes part of their daily routine.

Hyper-Local Presence
Reach consumers at the most intimate level — inside their own neighbourhood, at a store they visit multiple times per week.
Trust by Association
Spaza shops are deeply trusted community institutions. Brand association with these spaces transfers credibility to your product.
Exterior & Interior Branding
Full exterior painting, signage, and branded fascia — plus interior point-of-sale materials for complete brand immersion.

Digital OOH
Format 03

Digital Outdoor Advertising

Our digital outdoor screens bring the precision of digital marketing to the physical world. Run multiple creatives, schedule by time of day, react to live events, and update your campaign in real time — all without reprinting.

Ideal for brands running multi-product campaigns, time-sensitive promotions, or any activation that demands flexibility without sacrificing scale.

Real-Time Content Control
Update your creative, swap messaging, and respond to market conditions instantly — no production delays, no wasted print costs.
Dayparting & Scheduling
Schedule different creatives for morning commuters, lunchtime shoppers, and evening pedestrian traffic — maximising relevance.
High-Definition Displays
Full-colour HD LED panels with high brightness output — vibrant and legible in direct sunlight and at night.

Wall Mural
Format 04

Wall Murals

Wall murals transform blank exterior walls into monumental, culturally resonant brand statements. Larger than billboards, longer-lasting than digital, and inherently shareable — they create genuine landmarks in the communities they inhabit.

Unlike traditional advertising, murals become part of the visual fabric of a community. They generate organic social media amplification, local press coverage, and lasting brand recall that outlives any campaign flight.

Monumental Scale
Entire building facades transformed into unmissable brand canvases — visible from hundreds of metres in any direction.
Cultural Connection
Our designs are informed by local artistic traditions and community aesthetics — making your brand feel genuinely at home.
Organic Social Amplification
Striking murals become self-generating content — photographed and shared by community members, extending reach far beyond foot traffic.

Our Process

From Brief to Live Campaign

A straightforward, transparent process designed to get your brand in front of the right audiences as quickly and effectively as possible.

Step 01

Brief & Discovery

Share your campaign objectives, target audience, geography, and budget. We'll listen, ask the right questions, and define what success looks like for your brand.

Step 02

Site Recommendation

We present a curated selection of sites matched to your target demographic and campaign goals — with reach estimates, footfall data, and photo documentation for each location.

Step 03

Production & Installation

Your creative is produced to specification and professionally installed. We handle all logistics, site permissions, and quality assurance — you receive confirmation photography before launch.

Step 04

Campaign Monitoring

Throughout your flight, we monitor site conditions, provide proof-of-display photography, and maintain your creative — keeping your brand looking its best for the full campaign duration.
